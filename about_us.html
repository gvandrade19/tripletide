Triple Tide Story

At Triple Tide Surf School, surfing isn´t just a sport - it´s our life story. We´re three childhood friends who grew up riding the waves of Seixal, where the ocean became our playground and surfing our greatest passion. From catching our first waves as kids to competing in local surf contests, the sea has always been our second home.

After years of sharing unforgettable moments in the water, we decided to turn our love for surfing into something bigger. In 2024, we joined forces to create Triple Tide and our mission is simple: to share the stoke of surfing with everyone, from begginers to seasoned wave riders, and have a great time!

So, come join us in Seixal, where the waves are waiting, and the Triple Tide Family is ready to welcome you!
