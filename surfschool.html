Looking to enjoy a group surfing experience in Madeira? Triple Tide is the perfect choice!

Our lessons are tailored for groups of three or more participants. Please keep in mind that if there are individuals aged 10 or under in the group, an additional fee will apply for a coach to ensure their safety—because safety always comes first.

Info About the Lessons

Each lesson includes: Wetsuit, Surfboard, Leash, Wax, Fins, Rash guard, Insurance, Safety briefing and explanation of the surf spot, Practice on land.

What you should bring: Comfortable clothing for the day, bathing suit, towel, sunscreen, water, something light to eat, willingness to learn.

Meeting Point: @Cais do Seixal @Baía dos Juncos

Useful Tips

For better results and for you to get the most out of your surfing experience, we recommend you attend at least 5 consecutive lessons. Madeira Island may fulfil the perfect conditions for surfing, but the locals’ knowledge is crucial to prevent severe accidents.

Never try to surf on your own for the first time. Make sure the person accompanying you is an experienced surfer and knows the location of the surfing spots. When in doubt, ask for help, and always make sure you have the appropriate equipment and that the sea conditions are suitable for surfing.

Lesson Duration: The duration of the lesson is 120 minutes, ensuring plenty of time to learn and have fun!
